Hollow Knight

Hollow Knight is the first commercial game release by Australian studio Team Cherry, it is a side scrolling Metroidvania released in 2017 and received regular support through patches and expansions until August 2018.

Hollow Knight takes place in the Kingdom of Hallownest where you arrive with no clear goal or connection to the world, however through exploring you learn the goals of defeating the three dreamers spread throughout the world, and then eventually defeating the Hollow Knight.

The game features various secondary objectives and side quests that the player can go on to take a break from their main goal, such as finding all of the Grubs hidden through the world, or banishing the strange group of bugs called The Grimm Troupe

Hollow Knight features a truly incredible soundtrack created by Australian composer Christopher Larkin, when combined with the art of Team Cherry's Ari Gibson and game design of William Pellen, it makes for a truly unforgettable gameplay experience.
