Undertale is a 2D RPG developed by Toby Fox in 2015 and later received releases on home consoles through 2017 to 2021

Undertale features the story of a child named Frisk who falls into a hole in a mountain and ends up in the underground kingdom known as The Underground, throughout the game Frisk journeys through various fantastical locations such as The Ruins, Snowdin, Waterfall and Hotlands.

Undertale features various Monsters within the game, both as enemies and as Interactable NPCs that the player can form friendships with, Undertales characters are truly remarkable and leave a lasting impact on players who choose to engage with their stories.
