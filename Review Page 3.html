Warhammer 40,000 Rogue Trader

Warhammer 40,000 Rogue Trader is the, as of writing, latest game released by indie studio Owlcat Games and third game in total. Known primarily for their games in the Pathfinder setting and using the Pathfinder TTRPG ruleset, Rogue Trader is a large step from what they're known for, tackling an entirely different universe and a near original game system loosely based off of the TTRPG of the same name.

In Rogue Trader, you take the place of the latest Rogue Trader of the Von Valancius dynasty, as a Rogue Trader, you have vast amounts of power and freedom within the Warhammer 40,000 Setting which allows for the game to take you to places and see things that would ordinarily be near impossible within the setting.

Cut off from the worlds of your new domain, and with the last head of the Dynasty recently murdered, it is your task to reuinite your fracturing dynasty while withstanding attacks on all sides, strange alien beings have found ways to steal stars, the forces of Chaos are attacking worlds to fulfill a dire prophecy and the crew on your ship are constantly on the verge of rebellion.

The tension, mystery, stakes and mechanics of Rogue Trader all come together beautifully, you feel powerful when fighting weaker enemies but the game never hesitates to show you that there are things much stronger than you could imagine, it is another brilliant game from one of the best developers in the CRPG genre, and a must play for fans of Warhammer 40,000
